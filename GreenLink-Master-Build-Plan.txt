TEE SHEET UX REBUILD (phased initiative, frontend-only unless noted)
Audit baseline: 2026-04-08 — benchmarked against GolfManager and Lightspeed Golf
Architecture constraint: frontend only consumes TeeSheetDayResponse; no backend
  contract changes unless a phase explicitly states otherwise.

records the existing booking lifecycle and move UX as live, but the sheet still
